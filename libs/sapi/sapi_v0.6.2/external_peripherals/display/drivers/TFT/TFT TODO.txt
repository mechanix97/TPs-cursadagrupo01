Common TFT display controllers:

 - ST7735.
 - ILI9341.
 - ILI9163C.
 - HX8357 (320X480PX).
 - Samsung S6D02A1 (QDTech).
 
Communication:

 - SPI.

Libraries:

 - Adafruit ILI9341
 - Adafruit ST7735
 - Adafruit HX8357
 - Zigwart QDTech
 - Sumotoy ILI9163C

To make graphics all of these libs uses Adafruit GFX.
